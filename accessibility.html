Accessibility Statement

A-1 Action Safe & Lock is committed to making our website as accessible and usable as reasonably possible for all users, including individuals with disabilities.

We strive to follow generally accepted accessibility best practices and standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), to improve usability for people using a wide range of devices, browsers, and assistive technologies.

We review our website periodically and make reasonable efforts to improve accessibility over time. However, some content or functionality—especially third-party integrations such as maps, review widgets, or embedded media—may not be fully accessible.
